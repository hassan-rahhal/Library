About Bookify

BookBuddy is a simple digital library built for book lovers who want to explore, share, and organize their favorite reads in one place. Designed by university students, this project brings together clean design, interactivity, and accessibility — all using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Our Mission

We aim to create a platform where readers can easily discover new books, track what they’ve read, and stay inspired to read more. Our goal is to make digital libraries feel personal, friendly, and easy to use — even for casual readers.
